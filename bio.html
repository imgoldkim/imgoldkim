About

EN
Gold Kim is an artist working between Geneva and London. With a background in contemporary music and fine arts, their multidisciplinary practice spans sound, film, writing, and performance. Davin poetically explores the interconnectedness of life, addressing themes such as social inequality, human interaction, and the systems that shape our world. As a curator, they collaborate closely with other artists to develop new artistic genres and collective platforms. At the core of their work is a deep interest in the human senses—especially hearing. Through the act of sound collection, Gold investigates how they, others, and the world are connected through sound, listening attentively to the subtle voices of nature in a sensory and reflective approach.
